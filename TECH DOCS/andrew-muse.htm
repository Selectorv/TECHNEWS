OAHU AN EPIC ADVENTURE

We spent 5 incredible days seeing and experiencing some of the most beautiful places on Oahu based out of Waikiki. We started off by checking out one of the most pristine beaches with the clearest water I have ever seen. After that, I got to experience free diving with sharks thanks to One Ocean Diving. The following morning we went for a sunrise hike at Crouching Lion, where I scared myself scrambling up some sketchy rocks. Then we met up with local diver Adventure Man Dan for some cliff jumping at Spitting Caves. Our last day of the project we did some kiteboarding for my first time ever in Hawaii before watching an incredible sunset. This was an all time incredible experience!

Facts About Animal Fat and its Benefit

Examples

Animal fats are Saturated fats which are considered to increase total cholestrol in your body. For that reason, most nutrition experts recommend limmiting saturated fats.


Other Examples of Saturated Fats Are

1. Tropical oils (coconut oil, palm oil, cocoa butter)

2. High fat dairy foods whole milk, butter, cheese, sour cream, Yogurt (not all yogurt, look at Greek yogurt)which has been strained reulting in lower fat content but high in protein content.

3. Untrimmed beef, Pork and lamb

4. Paultry skin

10 Benefits of Animal Fat In Your Diet

1. Boost Immune System

Your immune system will function much better if you consume enough saturated fats
They contain vitamin A, D in animal fat and Vitamin E in vegetable fat which are essential nutrients to human body.


2. Nutrients Absorption

The body requires saturated fats to absorb other nutrients like proteins and vitamins and animal fats being the source of saturated fats it will help the body organs to maintain body cells functioning.


3. Release and Production of Bile

If you dont consume enough fats your body will not be able to produce enough Bile which aids the process of digestion and absorption of fats in the duodenum.


4.Bones

Strong bones are formed by vitamin D, apart from being manufactured in the skin on exposure to sunlight fat-soluble vitamins are required for normal bone development aiding to fetal growth, prevention of rickets and osteoporosis.


5. Growth

Pregnant women should consider taking enough animal fats for better fetal development, it also helps lactating mother to produce enough milk which is considered to be the best essential food with high nutrients for infants.


6. Hormonal balance

Few examples of hormones being Estrogen, testosterone, progesterone are all made up of fats, consuming animal fats helps in maintaining Hormonal balance. Have you considered yourself having mood swings, feeling anxious or being depressed, you are advised to increse the level of animal fat in your diet.


7. Keeps away hunger

Eating right portion of fat a day will make your stomach feel full thus reduce chance of overeating which is good for regulating body mass index (BMI).


8. Skin and Hair

As the saying goes, "You are what you eat". Skin is considered to be the largest body organ and it requires enough protains and fats for it to look healthy, It slows down aging of your skin.The dermatologist consider adding animal fats to your diet If you want beautiful youthful skin. Another one is you hair, if it lacks animal fats it will look dry and brittle.


9. Enhances Your Brain Fanctionality

Large portion of brain is made up of fats. Omega-3 fatty acids activates level of memory, focus, concentration etc. It is adviced that you should take enough fats to avoid memory loss in older age. Apart from Omega-3 fats from fatty fish such as salmon, mackerel and sardines,in plants seeds like soybean oil, flaxseeds, walnuts and canola oil you can also get from Eggs and saturated fats.


10. Gut Health

Autoimmune disorder is where one's immune system attacks ones own tissue.Experience of tremendous rumbling and bloating of the stomach leads to one's discomfort.You can avoid this by consuming fats.


Trans fat

It is unsaturated fat They are prepared by partial hydrogenation:that turns healthy oil into solid fat to prevent them from becoming rancid thus making them cheap. Which is associated to cause diseases like coronary heart disease, cancer, type 2 diabetes(science has proven to be silent killer)

Trans fats are used large amount in preparing fast-food eg French fries,baking and making pastries which are full of chalories.

Eating foods rich in trans fat increases cholestrol

But fats from Vegetables, nuts, seeds and fish are considered to fall under polysaturated and monosaturated fats which are good fats.

Introduction

1. What is Jackfruit

Jackfruit is a large and fleshy sweet fruit grouped under Moraceae family. Some can weigh over 38 kg, it has spiky thick outer skin which is green or mixture of green, yellow and brown when ripe. Large part of its flesh contains fiber like long and narrow pieces which are sticky. It is mainly grown in tropical areas.It is largely planted in Asia, originated from India also found in Africa and South America.

How To Prepare It

Apply olive oil on your hands or put on gloves so that it can not stick to your hands.

Use a sharp knife to cut into four pieces

Remove the seed and its pod gently(it has many seeds and each seed has a pod) and the separete the seed from its pod

You can cut the rest into your desired size.
